What does it mean to be at your desk?

In today's global and virtual work environment an employee's "desk" can be anywhere, challenging the traditional idea of an office cubicle farm.

Introducing ProMazo

ProMazo extends this idea of the mobile workforce and enables companies to tap into a completely untouched pool of talent: undergraduate and graduate students.

Helping Companies

Through ProMazo's system, companies will not only be able to complete projects, but can also test drive potential employees to ensure their qualifications and fit.
