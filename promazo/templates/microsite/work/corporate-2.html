How we helped to ensure their next generation product was ready for the field

KMC is a leading HVACC controls supplier that has recently teamed with Intel and Dell to deliver a cloud-based revolution in building heating and air systems. Their most recent product, Conquest, has just been released into the field and will begin worldwide deployment over the next year.

Ensuring KMC's Conquest system was ready for its debut

KMC needed to validate its software before the worldwide launch of its new control system. KMC turned to ProMazo to complete the testing of their software over the course of several months. We were required to bring together a diverse team to ensure that both techinical and non-technical employees could understand how to implement the system in the field.
